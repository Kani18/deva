How Dealerships Can Profit from Car Rental Revenue Streams

Anu  |  27.07.2020 10:10  |  15 comments

A slowdown in new auto sales the past few years has had dealerships opting to rent out the vehicles they have on showroom floors as a rental stream. And with the Coronavirus affecting with the automaker market, it’s not surprising that vehicle retailers are relying more on rentals to meet the bottom line. Given the uncertainty over how the pandemic will further manifest itself and its effect on a shaky economy, it won’t be surprising to see more dealerships jump onto the rental bandwagon. Auto Rental News, the house organ of the American Car Rental Association, recently unloaded a tip sheet for dealers wanting to exercise that rental option.
