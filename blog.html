Tamil  |  27.07.2020 10:10  |  15 comments

We know that the global auto industry faced vulnerable effects of the COVID-19 pandemic since its inception. The industry experienced a standstill for a period of time with control measures placed by Governments to control the virus spread. However, the large-scale global vaccination rollouts saw an upscale turn in the industry’s performance over the past few months. Success stories of companies are too being heard during the pandemic era. One such was Toyota being able to overtake Volkswagen as the bestselling automaker in 2020 for the first time in 5 years. Toyota attributed its success to implementing COVID-19 preventive measures and cooperative working patterns between suppliers and dealers.
